Current Research

I am currently contributing to the European project INFANT, which aims to develop intelligent and adaptive frameworks for pediatric healthcare and education. My work focuses on the design of interactive simulations and reasoning modules that model adaptive behavior and social interaction.

Within INFANT, I collaborate with interdisciplinary teams to integrate symbolic reasoning and machine learning into AI systems that can interact meaningfully with users and provide interpretable outcomes. This approach bridges rigorous formal logic with human-centered design.
