Research

Scientific vision, current lines, and collaborations.

Scientific Vision

My research focuses on formal reasoning and machine learning, aiming to develop neurosymbolic systems that can reason, adapt, and explain their behavior. I study how temporal and strategic logics enable transparent AI in interactive, educational, and humanâ€“machine environments.

Neurosymbolic Reasoning

Integration of temporal logics (ATL/CTL) with deep learning to combine symbolic precision and data-driven flexibility for planning and verification.

Simulation and Digital Twins

Interactive simulations and digital twins modeling adaptive agents and decision processes for healthcare, education, and sustainable technologies.

Explainability and Human Alignment

Methods to trace and interpret AI decisions, fostering accountability and trust in complex autonomous systems.

Logic-Based Game Environments

Formal verification and model checking in videogame AI to study intelligent behavior, fairness, and narrative consistency.

Collaborations and Open Directions

Collaboration with the Astrea Lab on formal logic and multi-agent reasoning, and with interdisciplinary groups in healthcare and education. Ongoing work connects strategic reasoning with interactive learning environments, targeting transparent AI grounded in logic and simulation.
