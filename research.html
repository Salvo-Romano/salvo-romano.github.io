Scientific Vision

My research focuses on the intersection of formal reasoning and machine learning, aiming to develop neurosymbolic systems that can reason, adapt, and explain their behavior. Neurosymbolic Reasoning

Integration of temporal logics (ATL/CTL) with deep learning models to enable hybrid reasoning that combines symbolic precision with data-driven flexibility.

Simulation and Digital Twins

Design of interactive simulations and digital twins that model adaptive agents and decision processes for healthcare, education, and sustainable technologies.

Explainability and Human Alignment

Methods for tracing, visualizing, and interpreting AI decisions, to ensure accountability and trust in complex autonomous systems.

Logic-Based Game Environments

Use of formal verification and model checking in videogame AI to explore intelligent behavior, fairness, and narrative consistency.

Collaborations and Open Directions

I collaborate with the Astrea Lab on formal logic and multi-agent reasoning, and with interdisciplinary groups in healthcare and education. Future work aims to connect strategic reasoning with interactive learning environments, fostering transparent AI systems grounded in logic and simulation.
